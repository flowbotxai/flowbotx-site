Founder

Meet Chris Pearce

Chris Pearce — Founder & CEO

Chris ran Incidents Paintless Dent Removal for 28 years in Michigan. During that time he did all of his own marketing and was consistently ranked first on Google across his entire service area. He didn’t hire an agency — he learned what worked by testing, measuring, and iterating on his own campaigns for nearly three decades.

That hands-on experience — knowing what it takes to actually show up online, capture leads, and close jobs as a service business owner — is the foundation of Snap Flow Solutions. Chris understands the daily reality: you’re on the job, calls go unanswered, follow-up gets delayed, your Google profile goes stale, and competitors who respond faster win work they shouldn’t.

After relocating to Ringgold, GA, Chris focused on building AI-powered marketing systems that solve the exact problems he lived for nearly three decades: missed calls, slow follow-up, weak online presence, and losing customers to competitors who respond faster. He combined his deep marketing expertise with modern AI automation to create done-for-you systems that run 24/7 without adding to a business owner’s workload.

Snap Flow Solutions now serves businesses throughout the greater Chattanooga metro area. Chris is an active member of local business networks including BNI, and is committed to helping service businesses in the region grow through smarter marketing and AI-powered automation.

How We Work

Our Approach: Audit First, Build Second

Before we touch a single system, we audit your entire digital footprint across nine categories: your website, Google presence, phone and communication, lead follow-up speed, social media, reviews and reputation, repeat business systems, CRM and pipeline, and AI search visibility.

You get a clear, honest report showing exactly what’s working, what’s broken, and what it’s costing you — before you spend a dollar. From there, we recommend a plan that fits your business and your budget, and we handle the entire build and ongoing management.

This audit-first approach means we never sell you something you don’t need. Every recommendation is based on data from your actual business, not a one-size-fits-all package.
